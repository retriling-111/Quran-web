About Us

Welcome to the Qur'an Web App, your digital companion for exploring the Holy Qur'an. Our mission is to provide an accessible, beautiful, and feature-rich platform for Muslims around the world to read, listen to, and connect with the words of Allah.

This project began on September 13, 2025, with the humble intention of creating a tool that is both modern and spiritually uplifting. We believe that technology can be a powerful medium for strengthening one's faith.

Our Vision

We aim to continuously improve this app by adding more features like tafsir, memorization tools, and community features, Insha'Allah. We are dedicated to ensuring the accuracy of the text and providing a seamless user experience.
